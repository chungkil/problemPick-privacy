ProblemPick Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2024

1. Introduction

At ProblemPick, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

Essential Information

  • Device identifier
  • Firebase authentication information
  • Storage access permission
  • Camera access permission

Optional Information

  • Photo gallery access permission

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Enable photo capture and storage features
  • Manage problem images and tags
  • Improve service quality and fix bugs
  • Enhance user experience
